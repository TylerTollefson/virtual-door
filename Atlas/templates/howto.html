How to Use the Office Door

To add widgets to your door:

  1. Click on the "Door Options" navbar element on the "Your Door" page.
  2. The widget select window will drop down from the top.
  3. Select the widget you want to put on the page.
  4. Click "Save Layout" under "Door Options" in the navbar to save your door's layout.

To remove a widget from your door, click this icon and click "Save Layout" under "Door Options" in the navbar.

Once a widget is on the page you can drag it to where you want it.
The widget can also be resized to a custom size.
To save your current layout once you have widgets on your door, go into door options and then click "save layout".
If you don't save your layout, the widgets you added will not be shown on your page the next time you visit.
To add/edit content in widgets look for either a or an , once clicked pop ups will appear and allow you to edit the widget's content
Widgets can be removed from pages by clicking the icon in their right corner, once a widget/multiple widgets have been removed you must save the layout.

Reminder: You must always save your layout after moving or resizing widgets!
